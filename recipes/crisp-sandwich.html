Crisp Sandwich


This one's for you if you think toast sandwich was tasteless.


Ingredients

  • A bag of crisps of your preference (also known as chips if you speak freedom english)
  • Two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Open the bag of crisps.
  2. Place the crisps in between the two slices of bread. Can't get simpler than that.
