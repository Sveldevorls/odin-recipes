Chip Butty


...And finally this one's for you if you didn't like the crisp sandwich either.


Ingredients

  • A bag of frozen chips (also known as french fries if you speak freedom english)
  • Two slices of bread
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Deep fry some chips.
  2. Season the chips with vinegar.
  3. Place the seasoned chips in between two slices of untoasted bread.
