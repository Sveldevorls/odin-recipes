Toast Sandwich


Description

Toast sandwich is the simplest yet tastiest sandwich you'll ever have, 100% guaranteed.


Ingredients

  • 3 pieces of bread
  • Thin slices of butter

    • Optional:
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toast a piece of bread in a toaster.
  2. Butter the toasted piece of bread.
  3. Put the toast in between the other two pieces of bread. You may spice it up with some salt and pepper if you perfer.
