Toast Sandwich


Toast sandwich is the simplest yet tastiest sandwich you'll ever have, 100% guaranteed.


Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread
  • Thin slice of butter
    • Optional:
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toast a slice of bread in a toaster.
  2. Butter the toasted piece of bread.
  3. Put the toast in between the other two slices of bread. You may spice it up with some salt and pepper if you perfer.
